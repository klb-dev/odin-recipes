Easy Chicken Parmesan

Chicken parmesan, also known as chicken parmigiana, is a classic Italian restaurant favorite. This dish actually originated from Italian immigrants in the US in the â€™50s and is thought to have originated from Eggplant Parmesan, a traditional Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Chicken Breast
  • Parsley
  • Marinara
  • Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Bread the chicken breasts and lightly brown them on each side
  2. Place them into a casserole dish with a bit of marinara and top with cheese.
  3. Bake until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
  4. Once it comes out of the oven, be sure to sprinkle with fresh basil and parsley.
